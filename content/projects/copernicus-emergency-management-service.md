External Consultant - Senior Software Developer | Joint Research Centre (JRC), Ispra | Copernicus Emergency Management Service, E1 Floods

Software analysis and development for Web/GIS applications supporting the Copernicus Emergency Management Service flood systems.

I work on the development and maintenance of EFAS and GloFAS websites and other web applications in the Floods section, contributing GIS, backend, frontend, and operational development expertise.

I also developed and manage the [Global Flood Partnership](https://www.globalfloodpartnership.org) website.

The work is carried out in collaboration with ECMWF-hosted repositories and workflows, using the Atlassian suite for project management, documentation, CI, and version control.

Stack: Python, Django, Go, PHP Drupal 7/8, Docker, Kubernetes, JavaScript ES6/ES7, React, Redux, AngularJS, GraphQL, OpenLayers, Leaflet, jQuery, PostgreSQL, PostGIS, Atlassian Bamboo, JIRA, Confluence, Bitbucket.
